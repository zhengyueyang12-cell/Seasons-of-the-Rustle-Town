feat(equipment): add hotbar weapons, tools, and held-item visuals

Architecture (data-driven equipment)
------------------------------------
resources/weapons/*.tres   weapon stats and sprites
resources/tools/*.tres     hoe, pickaxe, axe configs
WeaponRegistry / ToolRegistry   scan folders at startup

Player components
-----------------
EquipmentController   hotbar 1-8 selects weapon OR tool
WeaponManager         shows sword sprite in hand
ToolManager           shows hoe/axe/pickaxe sprite
CombatController      left-click attack (disabled when tool active)
ToolController        E key to use tool (hoe digs, axe chops trees)
HeldItemVisual        flip held item based on facing direction

Removed legacy wooden_sword.gd in favor of unified item_pickup.gd

Controls
--------
1-8 hotbar | left-click attack with weapon | E use tool
